The Conventicle of Chaos

Convention Created Content for Tier 1 Released by Baldman Games on July 15, 2018

After years of strife, Phlan is entering a new era of prosperity. Under the strong leadership of Jhessail Greycastle, external dangers have been beaten back, the city defenses have been strengthened, and the guilds are flourishing. But fresh threats have arisen within the city walls. Strange beasts stalk the night, bringing swift death in their wake, whilst a new criminal gang is vying for control of the streets. Can our heroes uncover the truth before more lives are lost?
