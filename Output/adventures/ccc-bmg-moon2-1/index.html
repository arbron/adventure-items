Defenders of Caer Moray

Convention Created Content for Tier 1 Released by Baldman Games on February 15, 2019

The most untamed of the Moonshaes, residents on Moray are accustomed to defending themselves. Recently something has stirred the dark forces that threaten the families of this strategically vital location. With every spare sword allocated to the homelands, who will answer their call for aid?

Part One of the Rising Shadows series.

Story Awards

Defenders of Caer Moray

Your deeds in Caer Moray are known to all and the citizens love you. You may re-roll any charisma-based roll once per session if you’re dealing with someone from Caer Moray.

A Friend in Caer Moray Downtime

You have a friend in Caer Moray. Write their name down and what they do next to this reward. As long as you continue to spend one out of every 10 downtime days to maintain your friendship you can ask your friend for a favor once per game session. The extent of this favor is the discretion of the Dungeon Master.
