Broken Blade

A 2–4 Hour Convention Created Content for Tier 2 Released on March 27, 2019

On the eve of the Red War, the High Blade of Mulmaster has become incapacitated by a mental assault. To give the city a fighting chance, you must help Selfaril Uoumdolphin confront the shadows of his past and free him from a century-long hatred.

Part Two of the Reborn Flame series.

Available on Dungeon Masters Guild.

Story Awards

A Lich’s Secret

When going through Selfaril’s memories of the past, you discovered Rastol Shan’s secret. He is a lich who was formerly Thurndan Tallwand, the Senior Cloak who betrayed Selfaril Uoumdolphin on behalf of his brother Rassendyll. What you do with this secret is up to you.
