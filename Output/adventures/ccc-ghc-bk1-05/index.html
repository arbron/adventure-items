For the Glory of Bloutar!

Convention Created Content for Tier 2 Released by Gamehole Publishing on December 6, 2018

You are told that some terrible evil has taken up residence underneath the ruins of a long abandoned keep and is now actively collecting artifacts of the world of the living for some unknown purpose.
