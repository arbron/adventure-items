Minauros Unchained

Convention Created Content for Tier 1 Released by Greasy Snitches on March 2, 2020

The valkyrie Sigrdri is looking for adventurers to go with her to the Nine Hells. Tasked to free heroic souls that are supposed to go to Ysgard but instead are stuck somewhere in different layers of Hell. Her first mission is to save a loyal and fearless minotaur druid that’s currently trapped in Minauros, the 3rd layer of Hell. Part One of Heroes in Nine Hells.
