Friends in Need

A 4 Hour Convention Created Content for Tier 2 Released on September 8, 2018

Faction agents have braved the Glacier of the White Worm and arrived at the Monastery of the Yellow Rose, only to be denied entry. It’s up to you and your companions to secure their entry, but it’s sure to be a wild ride.

Story Awards

Chapped

Your scarred legs and odd gait, gained from being burned astride a remorhaz, are easily recognized by monks at the Monastery of the Yellow Rose as the mark of foolishness or lack of balance. You make Charisma (Intimidate and Persuasion) checks against those monks with disadvantage.

Wormspur

Your boots are scarred and pocked with melted iron from destroyed wormspurs. Monks at the Monastery of the Yellow Rose recognize this as a sign of your remorhaz-riding prowess. You are more likely to gain entry to the monastery due to your prowess and you make Charisma (Intimidation and Persuasion) checks with advantage against monks who recognize your prowess.
