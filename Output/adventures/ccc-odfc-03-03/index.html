The Cause of All Life's Problems

Convention Created Content for Tier 3 Released on July 19, 2020

Riswyn Rumnaheim, a young female dwarf, has recently discovered that her sister has survived the Rage of Demons, but alas, this sister has been tainted by the Abyssal presence of the demon that owns her soul. She is on the lookout for adventurers to help her remove the taint, allowing her to recover at least some of the family that she lost. The characters will discover how to cleanse the soul and in so doing put down a rising evil that may threaten the surface.

Story Awards

Friend of the Clan Rumnaheim

As a Friend of Clan Rumnaheim, you can request entrance to the Earthspur Mines and Town, even if they are currently closed to outsiders. If Riswyn, new head of Clan Rumnaheim, is present at the mines (at the discretion of the DM), she can vouchsafe your good behavior and have you granted entrance. If you abuse this trust (at the discretion of the DM), void this story award.

Honorary Member of Clan Rumnaheim

You are considered an honorary member of the Rumnaheim clan. On your interactions within the Earthspur Mines and Town you are considered a dwarf, even if you are of a different race. For better or for worse.
