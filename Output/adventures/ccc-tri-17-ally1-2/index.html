Ph'theev, Unbound!

Convention Created Content for Tier 1 Released by The Role Initiative on September 16, 2018

This menace is still in its infancy, and must be taken care of before it poses a threat to the area-at-large. Your flumph friend must come with you to sniff out the signs, so keep it safe!
