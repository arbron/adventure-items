Where the Dead Wait

Oracle of War Adventure for Tier 1 Released on February 3, 2020

On their way back to Salvation, the adventurers are ambushed by a large force of undead and forced to seek shelter in a ruined cottage. As the dead close in from all sides, the survivors turn to the Oracle of War for a lifeline. Only this time, things don’t play out as expected… The third adventure in the four-part Spoils of War storyline.

Legacy Events

Stolen Artifact

A group of scavengers stole the Oracle of War from you. If you play DDAL-EB-04 The Third Protocol, you start that adventure without the Oracle. You recover the artifact for Tiers 2–4.

Turned Traitor

Your ally Kalli Alran (or the friend you created in DDAL-EB-01 The Night Land) betrayed you to steal the Oracle of War. If they escaped alive, they may show up again in future adventures.
