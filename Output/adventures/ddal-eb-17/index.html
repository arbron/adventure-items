The Final Tribute

A 4 Hour Oracle of War Adventure for Tier 4 Released on July 5, 2021

Prince Oargev of New Cyre knows the location of the secret Cyran creation forge, but a grim litany of recent disasters has shaken his throne: plague, treachery, recession, and insurgency. This intelligence could buy him a chance to rebuild anew, but who’ll be his highest bidder?

Part One of the Flames of War series.
