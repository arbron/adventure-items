The Rising City

A 4 Hour Oracle of War Epic for Tier 3 Released on June 20, 2021

In this Epic event, an army of symbiont-enhanced soldiers guards the Emerald Claw as they excavate the daelkyr sanctuary beneath Metrol. To get past them, the adventurers need a diversion. A gang of scavengers camped in the city are willing to help, but they have their own demands—a long list of them, in fact!

Available on Dungeon Masters Guild.
