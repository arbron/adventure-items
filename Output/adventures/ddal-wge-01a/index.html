The Callestan Clash

Embers of the Last War Adventure for Tier 1 Released on July 22, 2018

Life in Callestan is not all fun and games—except for those who make their own fun with their own games. Mischievous, hardy street dwellers known as the Callestan Clash live and lurk in the shadows of the district’s looming towers, reaching their fingers into the satchels of unsuspecting tourists or balling them into fists, readying a swing. While members of the Clash are scrappy and abrasive, they enjoy opportunities to compete against strangers—and earn some much-needed coin in the process.
