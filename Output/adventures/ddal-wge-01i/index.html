Possession is Nine-Tenths

Embers of the Last War Adventure for Tiers 1 and 2 Released on July 22, 2018

Goblins are found across Eberron, and while many are relegated to lower-class residences and employment, a few may find the strength (or allies) to ascend to a higher calling. In this series of encounters, the characters meet Stab, a resourceful goblin, and his faithful mount Rations. They may earn his loyalty and assist him on his “missions”, but unfortunately, Stab’s resourcefulness comes at a price.
