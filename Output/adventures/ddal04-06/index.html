Story Awards

The Demiplane of Dread

You have traversed the mists and now find yourself in the Demiplane of Dread and, until this story award is removed, you are unable to leave. So long as you are trapped, you cannot participate in any adventure or event that takes place outside of Barovia. Tread carefully during your time here, the Dark Powers are watching.

Enmity of Jeny Greenteeth

You don’t cross a hag like Jeny and get away without paying the price. For so long as you possess this story award, Jeny charges you 50% more for any spellcasting services that she provides. In addition, you may not use the alternate methods of payment detailed in the Adventurers League Dungeon Master’s Guide.

Escape from the Demiplane of Dread

You have been escorted out of Barovia by a family of Vistani. The trip is long and arduous, however. Once free, however, you must spend 80 downtime days traversing the width and breadth of Faerûn in search of the mysterious mists if you wish to return to Barovia. Once you have returned, you once more regain The Demiplane of Dread story award and must find a new way out of Barovia.
