The Broken One

Season 4 Adventure for Tier 2 Released on May 2, 2016

The village of Orașnou is buried in winter’s snows and facing starvation. An unseen beast has ravaged the livestock culled to pay Lord Strahd’s tax and the village is thrown into panic and chaos. Can you find the monster and save the village from starvation, or worse?

Part Eight of the Misty Fortunes and Absent Hearts series.

The Demiplane of Dread

You have traversed the mists and now find yourself in the Demiplane of Dread and, until this story award is removed, you are unable to leave. So long as you are trapped, you cannot participate in any adventure or event that takes place outside of Barovia. Tread carefully during your time here, the Dark Powers are watching.

You Murderer!

You have killed a local villager. As a result, you are distrusted (and likely even hated) by the survivors of Eugen’s attacks. For so long as you possess this story award, all of your Charisma (Deception or Perception) checks against a local of Orașnou are made with disadvantage. Additionally, all prices for any purchases or services in the village are doubled.
