A Blight in the Darkness

A 4 Hour Season 10 Adventure for Tier 2 Released on March 2, 2021

The wicked owlbear Frostclaw was nearly defeated. Now it’s time to finish the deed and destroy her and her master once and for all. But first, you must find them. Track them down and save Ten-Towns!

Part Five of the Plague of Ancients series.

Story Awards

Corrupted Chardalyn Infusion

Characters who fail their saving throw against the exploding chardalyn staff earn this story award.

Sing a Song of Vengeance

The characters earn this story award if they bring Feral-Tongue and Kaskur back to Wyrmdoom Crag alive.

The Song Falls Silent

The characters earn this story award if Kaskur dies during the adventure and isn’t returned to life.
