The Mystery of Placid Cay

A 4 Hour Author Only Adventure for Tier 1

A wealthy Amnian merchant is missing a highly valuable shipment of exotic fruit from an island in the Sea of Swords. She needs adventurers to travel to the island and see what might be happening there. Along the way one might expect an exciting sea voyage, a terrible curse, and a mystery on the idyllic island known as Placid Cay.
