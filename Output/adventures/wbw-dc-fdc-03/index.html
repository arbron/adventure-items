I Find That Familiar

A 4 Hour Dungeoncraft Adventure for Tier 1 Released on September 21, 2021

This is a disaster! Spellcasters all over the realm cannot summon their fey familiars. Something must’ve happened in Paliparan, the Domain of Delight where a lot of fey creatures go before they get summoned using the find familiar spell.

Content Warning

Gambling, animal harm, fey curses, child imprisonment, illness, loss of a friend

Available on Dungeon Masters Guild.
