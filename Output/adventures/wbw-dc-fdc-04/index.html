The Monster of Wonderia

A 2 Hour Dungeoncraft Adventure for Tier 1 Released on September 20, 2021

The adventure takes place in the new Feywild Domain of Delight, Wonderia, a magical land where childlike fey creatures called wonderfey live. In this adventure, the characters are summoned to Wonderia by wonderfey. The wonderfey ask the characters to serve as their guardians during their journey to the Forbidden Mountain where it is rumored the Monster of Wonderia lives.

Unbeknownst to the characters and the wonderfey, the Archfey of Wonderia, Abuelita Yvelise, resides there. Abuelita Yvelise is a sweet kindly storm giant who reveals the true nature of the wonderfey to the characters. The characters join the wonderfey in a ritual that takes place in the Archfey’s bag of holding where they must defeat vile fey creatures seeking to scare off the wonderfey from entering a portal that sends them to merge with each character’s soul at birth (time has no bounds in Wonderia!). The adventure concludes with the characters making choices that can completely reshape their own lives, including changing their race, class, gender, and other aspects of their character.

Available on Dungeon Masters Guild.
