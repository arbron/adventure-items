The Landscape of Estorian LeFae

A 2 Hour Dungeoncraft Adventure for Tier 1 Released on September 20, 2021

Canvas is a self-contained domain inside of a painting, protected by magic and housing secrets to be hidden in plain sight although in a place of art and creation, anything can become reality, and that in itself is dangerous as the ruler is a bit eclectic. This Domain of Pigment is located inside of a painting drawn of a large tree covered in moss, raised on the ground, and with water flowing out of the base forming a small waterfall. It is labeled "Season's Fleetings" by Estorian LeFae. The painting changes to reflect different seasons from an outside perspective as the domain ruler's position changes. Inside of the painting is a collection of stories and artwork unique to the fey. This domain is a self-contained archive although the archive may have been less preserved and more acquired in selfish desire.

Available on Dungeon Masters Guild.
