A Stitch in Time

A 4 Hour Dungeoncraft Adventure for Tier 1 Released on September 19, 2021

A clockwork Wonderland is running out of time as the gears that keep the domain ticking are in need of repair. The royal artificer, Hairspring, requests assistance on his first day on the job. As the adventuring party ventures into the Feywild, they meet some peculiar creatures with problems of their own—ones that can be solved through wordplay, riddles, and logic. The domain of Belleturnum awaits…don’t be late!
