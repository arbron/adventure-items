Exploratory Arts CCC-YLRA-SDCC-01-01

Anticipation is running high for the Ylraphon Convocation of Delvers, Seekers, and Sages, and every adventurer worth their salt is flocking to Ylraphon to take part. The festival promises tests of skill, renowned guest lecturers, an extensive Mercantile Exhibition, and many more attractions. But within this celebration of the exploratory arts lurks an uninvited guest of honor—danger!

No Good Deed CCC-YLRA-SDCC-01-02

The Ylraphon Convocation of Delvers, Seekers, and Sages is in full swing, and its Mercantile Exhibition is packed with festival-goers. But not all of them are here for bargains, as the characters soon discover, and good deeds may not be enough to bring peace to Ylraphon.

Cast In Shadow CCC-YLRA-SDCC-01-03

The many disasters plaguing the first-ever Ylraphon Convocation of Delvers, Seekers, and Sages seem to be the work of someone or something in the Flooded Forest. To put a stop to the festival’s troubles, the characters will brave the vast swamp and consult with the Wise Woman who lives there—and find themselves caught up in a sinister plot centuries in the making.
