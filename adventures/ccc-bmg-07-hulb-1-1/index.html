Story Awards

Gratitude of Hulburg’s Gangs

Write down on the campaign log the character earned the gratitude of one or more of the following gangs of Hulburg: Clan Stoneringer / Scarlet Fangs / Ship Guild. Scratch the names of those gangs the characters aggravated. In general, the gangs keep the whole group responsible for the behavior of one, but if any connection is unclear or characters go out of their way to apologize you can add or remove gangs from individual PC’s rewards.
