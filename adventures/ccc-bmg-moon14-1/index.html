Hearts Amiss

A 4 Hour Convention Created Content for Tier 2 Released by Baldman Games on September 2, 2021

The city of Karador is the crowning jewel of Sarifal, but Lady Siofra worries that it has been trapped in its cycle of winter for too long. She seeks those not of the court to investigate the fey city and find out what is keeping it from blooming into spring.
