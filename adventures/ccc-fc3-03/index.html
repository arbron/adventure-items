The Fun House

Convention Created Content for Tier 3 Released on March 11, 2019

Randel Malark Ambledragon was a magician of impressive renown. Rumour has it that before he became a travelling magician he a very successful adventurer. He settled down in Estirwald until one day he and his mansion simply disappeared. Now his mansion is back and rumours are flying all over the place. Are you courageous, daring, and clever enough to find out the truth? Part Three of the Tales of Estirwald.
