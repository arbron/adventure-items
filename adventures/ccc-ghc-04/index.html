Shade of the Dark

Convention Created Content for Tier 2 Released by Gamehole Publishing on December 3, 2017

Deep in the forests of Cormanthor, the Golden Paragon Trading Company has set up a logging camp to harvest the valuable trees of the Midwood. Until recently, the profitable camp delivered lumber downriver to Semberholme, but its last communication spoke of attacks from forest monsters and panicked loggers unwilling to work. The Golden Paragon Trading Company needs adventurers to find the underlying cause of their timber production problems. Are the incursions simply the woodland creatures defending their home, or is something more sinister happening in the ancient forest?
