Finglebruggle’s Phantomball Field

A 2–4 Hour Convention Created Content for Tier 2 Released by Gamehole Publishing on May 14, 2020

Don’t Always Root for the Home Team

The heroes are pulled into a fast-paced sport played in certain parts of the Border Kingdoms. But the trophy, the community’s pride and joy, is stolen by a vengeful wizard who challenges the party to retrieve it in his own private “Finglebruggleball“ dungeon. Can the heroes use their newly acquired sports skills to overcome the wizard’s deadly traps and mysteries?
