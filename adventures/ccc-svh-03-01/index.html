Finder's Fiend

Convention Created Content for Tier 2 Released on April 28, 2020

Souls bound to a deadly fiend have escaped the Nine Hells and taken over denizens of Thentia. The devil wants the souls back and if they're not delivered quickly, she intends to come for them herself, endangering more innocents. To save the lives of the unsuspecting hosts, adventurers must track them down and send the stray souls back to Hell. A Sisterhood of the Blade adventure.

Story Awards

Mark of the Sisterhood

You have earned some favor with the Sisterhood of the Blade bounty hunting organization. By trading in this mark to a member of the sisterhood, you get their attention and some measure of cooperation. Benefits bestowed are detailed in adventures featuring the sisterhood and remain unknown until the mark is used.
