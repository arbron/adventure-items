Dead Men’s Treasure

A 4 Hour Convention Created Content for Tier 2 Released by The Role Initiative on November 6, 2018

A map with an “X marking the spot” has been found in a recently uncovered tomb located in the small fishing village of Abigail’s Cove. The village’s mayor has sent out a secret call to trusted adventurers; offering a split of whatever treasure they can recover. Success means fortune for Abigail’s Cove, its villagers, and the adventurers.

Part Two of the Dead Men series.

Available on Dungeon Masters Guild.
