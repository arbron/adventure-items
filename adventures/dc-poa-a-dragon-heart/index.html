A Dragon Heart

A 4 Hour Dungeoncraft Adventure for Tier 1 Released on December 21, 2020

Adventure Seed: Once Bitten, Ice Shy

Three kobolds have been dreaming of becoming real dragonborns since childhood. They heard of a powerful entity that can make dreams come true, in exchange of a rare crystal found in a dungeon. One of the three kobold went there, but never came back. Please find him back, and discover why this crystal is so important.
