A 4 Hour Dungeoncraft Adventure for Tier 1 Released on February 11, 2021

Adventure Seed: The Warmest Day

A bright sphere of fire has appeared over the town of Termalaine. It showers the town in warmth and light as if it were the sun. The townsfolk celebrate what appears to a blessing from the gods. The truth is the false sun is a beacon conjured by an evil cursed artifact that has been awakened. Now hordes of undead pirates and bandits have seen this beacon and seek to claim the artifact for themselves.
