Adventure Seed: Snipe Hunt

Talk of a snipe, a legendary creature with magical feathers, comes to Lonelywood. It seems like nothing more than an interesting rumor, but it gives the people of Sunlit a great idea: a race to “hunt down” a snipe across the Lonelywood Forest. The game is on, all of Sunlit is excited, and you’re in on the fun. Let’s see what contests and circus acts pop up in this new solstice tradition.
