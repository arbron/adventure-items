Lord Bucket

Oracle of War Adventure for Tier 2 Released on August 31, 2020

When word reaches Breland that the Lord of Blades is plotting an attack, the outpost of Salvation becomes the first line of defense. In preparation for war, the Brelish army enlists the adventurers to secure the outpost, serving under the vainglorious General Karol Tyresh; a buffoon known to his troops as “Lord Bucket.” The second in a trilogy of adventures, the Dogs of War storyline.
