Dream Eater

Oracle of War Adventure for Tier 3 Released on April 5, 2021

Across Eberron, the spirits of the dead stir as the Order of the Emerald Claw draws back the veil to Dolurrh. To avert calamity, the adventurers must cut a deal with an infamous bonecaster of the Talenta Plains—the Dream Eater—and her pack of loyal tyrannosaurs. This is the second in a trilogy of adventures, the Engines of War storyline.

Dragon Spirit

In the mists of the Boneyard, you allowed the restless spirit of a silver dragon to latch onto your soul. While the dragon spirit dwells inside you, you are immune to the Frightful Presence of dragons and have resistance to damage from their breath weapons.

Dream Walker

You helped the Dream Eater lay the restless spirit of hte dragon Carnelian to rest. At the end of the adventure, choose one of the following rewards:

  • The Dream Eater performs a ritual that grants you resistance to necrotic damage for the duration of your next adventure.
  • If you chose the allosaurus mount as a reward, the halfling Norrin teaches it the “Talenta Dodge”. If the allosaurus is subjected to an effect that allows it to make a saving throw to take only half damage, it instead takes no damage if it succeeds on the saving throw, and only half damage if it fails. (Norrin must survive the adventure to offer this training.)
  • If you chose the bracers of archery reward, the halfling Davric enchants your quiver with a luck charm. When you roll a 1 on a d20 for an attack roll with an arrow drawn from this quiver, you can reroll the die and must use the new roll. (Davric must survive the adventure to offer this reward.)
