Rolling Thunder

Oracle of War Epic for Tier 2 Released on November 9, 2020

In this Epic event, the adventurers infiltrate the royal lightning rail train to steal a ledger belonging to the king’s brother, Kor ir’Wynarn. With war brewing between Breland and Thrane, Lord Kor rides north to Sword Keep to defend the border—but scheming forces intend to strike him down before he gets there.
