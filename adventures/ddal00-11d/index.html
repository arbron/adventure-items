Story Awards

Pipyap Owes You One

You have done a favor for the imp Pipyap and now he owes you one. You can cash in this favor in one of three ways:

  • Gain 150 gp.
  • Gain one consumable potion or scroll from Table A or B in the Dungeon Master’s Guide.
  • Pipyap arranges for your body to be recovered when otherwise thought lost. It’s best not to ask how he did it or where that strange smell came from. This favor goes both ways. Now that you have accepted something from the devil, he might call on you in the future to further your alliance.
