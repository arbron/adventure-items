Chelimber’s Descent

Season 5 Adventure for Tier 1 Released on December 5, 2016

Deep in the Marsh of Chelimber lie the ruins of a great wizard’s tower. SEER has received intelligence, which she imparts via her emissary Hsing, that deep beneath the marsh in the remains of an old tower is knowledge of older magics. This could be just what Parnast needs to resist the onslaught of Bad Fruul and his armies!

A Two-Hour Adventure for 1st–4th Level Characters.

Items

  • Wand of Webs Uncommon
  • 1 x Spell Scroll of Lesser Restoration Uncommon
  • 1 x Spell Scroll of Tongues Uncommon

Story Awards

Faction Copyist Downtime

Any character may spend ten downtime days assisting their faction by copying the texts retrieved for SEER. Upon completing this downtime activity, the character may copy one spell from the Elemental Evil Player’s Companion of a level they can cast for their spellbook.
