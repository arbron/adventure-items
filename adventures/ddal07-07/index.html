Rotting Roots

A 2 Hour Season 7 Adventure for Tier 2 Released on December 4, 2017

The sudden appearance of the undead within Port Nyanzaru doesn’t appear to be the only thing on the horizon. A vast horde of skeletons and zombies is moving towards the city and while those of means are safe within the city’s walls, those in the Old City and Malar’s Throat are forced to contend with the problem. Where did they come from? More importantly, what are you going to do to find out?

Part Two of The Rot from Within series.
