Story Awards

You Let Dara Die

Dara has now died _______ times while in your care. Every time she dies, Dara loses one of the following abilities, chosen at random: Damage Resistances, Condition Immunities, Magic Resistance, and Innate Spellcasting. If she loses all four, she has lost all of her abilities as a Chosen and may no longer return Hellriders or angels to life.

In future adventures that she’s featured in, your group uses the average number of deaths among the characters for determining how many abilities she has lost.
