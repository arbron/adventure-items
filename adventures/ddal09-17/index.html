Torm’s Chosen

For nobly giving yourself so that High Priest of Torm Horst Atheraice could be freed, Torm blessed a piece of your soul. The expedition is in peril as the opposition has gained key intelligence from you before killing you.

After suffering torture at the hands of General Everbleed’s devils, you died and are reborn in any location of your choice in Faerûn that has a temple to Torm, and the next time you die, you gain this benefit as well (as if a greater resurrection).
