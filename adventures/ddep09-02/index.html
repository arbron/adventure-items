Story Awards

Marked by Bel

You agreed to make a deal with Bel or one of his minions. Your soul is now forfeit; if you die, you can only be restored to life via a wish or true resurrection spell, after which you can remove this award. Similarly, you regain hit dice at half the normal rate. However, Bel has granted you some special powers in exchange for this self-sacrifice:
