Story Awards

Traded with a Hag

Either out of greed, naivety, or sheer ignorance, you traded with a hag and now bear a mark visible only to other fey. Any Charisma (Persuasion) checks made to positively influence non-evil fey are made with disadvantage. In order to remove this mark, the hag that you traded with must willingly return the "payment" you rendered in the trade.
