A Meal to Remember

A 2 Hour Dungeoncraft Adventure for Tier 1 Released on September 20, 2021

Dwindle is a domain of loss and loneliness: a vast desert realm where memory and mirage are one and the same. On the eve of a much-anticipated dinner, an elderly resident of Dwindle, Forough Khanom, awakes to find her vegetable garden trampled. To save her dinner, the characters must brave the dunes of the departed in search of ingredients from the Sabzi Knoll.

Content Warning

Grief, memory loss
